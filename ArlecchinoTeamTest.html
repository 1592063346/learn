Teams and Tests

Five teams I tested are as follows.

  • (Mono-pyro team 1) Arlecchino, Kazuha, Xiangling, Bennett
  • (Mono-pyro team 2) Arlecchino, Zhongli, Xiangling, Bennett
  • (Vaporize team 1) Arlecchino, Zhongli, Yelan, Bennett
  • (Vaporize team 2) Arlecchino, Xingqiu, Kazuha, Bennett
  • (2 pyro + 2 geo team) Arlecchino, Bennett, Navia, Zhongli

An interesting thing is, Bennett appears in all the teams without exception, because he, and 2 pyro resonance, do provide too much ATK bonus.

All subsequent tests were conducted in Abyss 12-1 (in version 4.6). The object was Maguu Kenki (HP: 1,109,954*3), and the benediction I chose was "Character Normal and Charged Attack DMG +20%".

It should be pointed out that, Maguu Kenki's HP here was actually not high, and our team often defeated them in a very short time. However, it is well known that the damage caused by Arlecchino generally declines smoothly over time, and the damage output in a short time in the early period is often positively correlated with the overall damage output in a full cycle time. The full cycle time of all Arlecchino's teams are almost the same. Therefore, we could simply use the time it took to defeat Maguu Kenki as one of our metrics.

Mono-pyro team 1

Team members: Arlecchino, Kazuha, Xiangling, Bennett.

The order of my operations was:

  1. Arlecchino E
  2. Xiangling E (This step is because my Xiangling was equipped with Staff of the Scarlet Sands)
  3. Kazuha Q + E
  4. Bennett Q
  5. Xiangling Q
  6. Arlecchino Charged Attack + Normal Attacks...

The highest damage Arlecchino dealt in one attack was 171,932. With the high damage provided by Xiangling's elemental burst, the battle was over in only 13s.

Mono-pyro team 2

Team members: Arlecchino, Zhongli, Xiangling, Bennett.

The order of my operations was:

  1. Arlecchino E
  2. Xiangling E
  3. Zhongli E
  4. Bennett Q
  5. Xiangling Q
  6. Arlecchino Charged Attack + Normal Attacks...

The highest damage Arlecchino dealt in one attack was 104,944, and the battle was over in 15s. Compared to the last team, this team exchanged the survival guarantee provided by Zhongli for the damage bonus provided by Kazuha.

You may wonder why the total battle time gap is only 2s when the highest damage is so different. In fact, don't forget that for the first half of the battle time, I only took turns switching characters and using skills (in this team, Zhongli didn't even use the elemental burst). When the enemy's HP increases and the battle time becomes longer, the gap between the two teams will become very obvious.

Vaporize team 1

Team members: Arlecchino, Zhongli, Yelan, Bennett.

The order of my operations was:

  1. Arlecchino E
  2. Zhongli E
  3. Yelan E + Q
  4. Bennett Q
  5. Arlecchino Charged Attack + Normal Attacks...

The highest damage Arlecchino dealt in one attack was 175,140, and the battle was over in 16s.

This result is understandable. Yelan's elemental burst can only damage the individual, so her performance was not excellent enough in the situation of enemy groups. The most direct manifestation was that the overall vaporization ratio is low. Although the battle ended in 16s, the first Maguu Kenki actually fell in 12s, which means that this team will perform well against individual enemy.

Vaporize team 2

Team members: Arlecchino, Xingqiu, Kazuha, Bennett.

The order of my operations was:

  1. Arlecchino E
  2. Xingqiu Q + E (+E, if Xingqiu was equipped with Sacrificial Sword)
  3. Kazuha E (Swirl hydro)
  4. Bennett Q
  5. Kazuha Q (Swirl pyro)
  6. Arlecchino Charged Attack + Normal Attacks...

The highest damage Arlecchino dealt in one attack was 263,242, and the battle was over in 14s.

Similarly, compared to the last team, this team exchanged the survival guarantee for the damage bonus. In this team, I chose Xingqiu over Yelan for several reasons. First, Rain Swords around Xingqiu also provide hydro attachment, and 18s duration of Xingqiu's elemental burst allows more possible vaporization damage than 15s duration of Yelan's elemental burst. Second, Xingqiu provides better protection for Arlecchino. For me, an added reason is that my Yelan is C0 and equipped with Favonius Warbow, so I also need to think about her energy in this team. Overall, this team performed quite well, assuming survival first of course.

Results

Based on the above tests, one general conclusion is clear. That is, if you care enough about the survival, then take Zhongli; if you are confident in your operation and want more damage, then take Kazuha.

There seems to be no clear principle for choosing between mono-pyro team and vaporize team due to the limitation of teams and enemies I tested. To a certain extent, choosing which team depends on the type of enemy. For example, vaporize team might perform better in the situation of individual enemy (like BOSS). I use "might" here, because some mono-pyro teams actually perform well against individual enemy too. Some vaporize teams can also deal with enemy groups, as long as we take characters who can provide a large area of hydro attachment like Ayato, but those teams are traditional and don't play to Arlecchino's true strength. Choosing which team also depends on what characters you have, and this is probably the most important factor. For example, above "2 pyro+2 geo" team is a good compromise choice in case of character conflict.

There are also some teams I don't mention, including overloaded team and melt team. For overloaded team, I didn't build Chevreuse, Fischl and Beidou, so I cannot give a comment. For melt team, I think it's not very realistic in existing characters.
